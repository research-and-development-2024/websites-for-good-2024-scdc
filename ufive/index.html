Developing a Digital Business

Small businesses and startups in Southeast Los Angeles is able to engage with the Southeast Community Development Corporation's digital services from their expertise in providing low-cost digital services and training that can contribute to the success of entrepreneurs in the region.

Business Digital Services

Digital Literacy Skills & Training

SCDC can offer training courses to local businesses to develop their skills in digital market by using digital technologies and tools.

Building Professional Websites

SCDC creates a digital platform to demonstrate the platform online. SCDC has a goal to serve as a digital storefront by offering details such as services, and contact information.

Creating Digital Menus

SCDC can provide resources on how to implement digital menus for use in many types that local businesses can find useful.
